Abstract

The Neighbourhood Photovoltaic Generation Ontology (NPO) aims to consolidate diverse photovoltaic (PV) generation data at the neighbourhood scale. Its goal is to support the estimation of PV generation and informed decision-making for micro-scale sustainable urban planning initiatives.
